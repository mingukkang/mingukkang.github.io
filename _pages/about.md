I am a Founding Research Scientist at [Pika](https://www.pika.me/), where I work on efficient generative AI systems for video, audio, and multimodal content generation. I received my Ph.D. from the [Graduate School of AI](https://ai.postech.ac.kr/) at [POSTECH](https://postech.ac.kr/eng/) in 2026, advised by Prof. [Suha Kwak](https://suhakwak.github.io) (2023--2026) and Prof. [Jaesik Park](https://jaesik.info/) (2020--2023). Previously, I interned at [Adobe Research](https://research.adobe.com/) with [Taesung Park](https://taesung.me/), [Connelly Barnes](https://www.connellybarnes.com/work/), [Eli Shechtman](https://research.adobe.com/person/eli-shechtman/), [Jun-Yan Zhu](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~junyanz/), [Richard Zhang](https://richzhang.github.io/), and [Sylvain Paris](https://research.adobe.com/person/sylvain-paris/), where my research contributed to the development of [Adobe Firefly](https://www.adobe.com/products/firefly.html). I received my B.S. in Engineering from [Pusan National University](https://www.pusan.ac.kr/).

At Pika, I have contributed to [PikaStream1.0](https://www.pika.me/blog/introducing-real-time-video-chat), Nova lipsync models, and Pika video generation models including Pika 1.5, 2.0, 2.1, and 2.2. My work spans tokenizers, distillation pipelines, fast super-resolution methods, and real-time video agent system components.

My research focuses on efficient generative AI systems for real-time content generation and understanding across video, audio, and multimodal settings. I work on low-latency tokenizers, diffusion-model distillation for few-step synthesis, and fast super-resolution, with broader interests in GAN and diffusion-model pre-training, tokenizer diffusibility, and scalable multimodal models.
