Minguk Kang (강민국)

I am a Founding Research Scientist at Pika. I received my Ph.D. from the Graduate School of AI at POSTECH in 2026, advised by Prof. Suha Kwak and Prof. Jaesik Park. Previously, I was a Research Scientist Intern at Adobe Research, where my work on GigaGAN contributed to Adobe Firefly. I received my B.S. from Pusan National University.

At Pika, I have contributed to PikaStream1.0, the Nova lipsync model, and Pika video generation models including Pika 1.5, 2.0, 2.1, and Pika 2.2. My work spans tokenizers, diffusion distillation, fast super-resolution, and components for real-time video agent systems.

My research focuses on efficient generative modeling for real-time content generation across video, audio, and multimodal media. I am particularly interested in high-compression, low-latency tokenizers, few-step diffusion distillation, fast super-resolution, tokenizer design and diffusibility, and multimodal generative modeling.

Products & Software

PikaStream1.0: core contributor to a real-time video agent system for group video chat, focusing on low-latency generation and multimodal capabilities.

Nova Lipsync Model: developed generation and acceleration pipelines for the Nova lipsync model.

Pika Video Generation Models: contributed to Pika 1.5, Pika 2.0, Pika 2.1, and Pika 2.2, with work on tokenizers, distillation, and fast super-resolution.

E-LatentLPIPS is a latent-space perceptual metric for Latent Diffusion Models, enabling regression tasks without pixel decoding.

Adobe Firefly is Adobe’s visual generative AI product suite; my GigaGAN research contributed to its development.

PyTorch StudioGAN is an open-source PyTorch library for representative GAN training and evaluation.
